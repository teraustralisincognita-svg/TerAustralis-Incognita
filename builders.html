Builders · CrystalCore

Knowledge that travels like a songline.

CrystalCore is the reasoning layer beneath TerAustralis — a sovereign, local-first substrate where every conclusion carries where it came from, who may hold it, and how far it can be trusted.

What it is

CrystalCore is a foundation layer — a sovereign reasoning substrate. It is not a finished AGI, and it is not a web-security product. It is the auditable groundwork a larger system can one day stand on — built right from the start, so it holds.

Local-first

It stays with you

Runs offline on modest hardware — an old laptop, a small server, a Raspberry Pi — with zero required dependencies. Your data stays close to Country.

Auditable

It shows its working

No black box. Every conclusion can be traced back through the exact steps and sources that produced it.

Consent-first

It asks before it acts

Reasoning fails closed: hand it something a reader isn't permitted to see, and the chain stops rather than leaking.

What travels the line

Three things ride every songline.

A songline carries more than a route — it carries who the knowledge belongs to and how it must be passed on. CrystalCore carries the same three things through every step of reasoning.

01

Provenance

Every derived fact links back to the inputs and the rule that made it. Nothing arrives without a trail you can follow home.

02

Consent

A reader sees only what its permissions allow. Reasoning fails closed on a forbidden input — it stops rather than guessing past the boundary.

03

Coherence

Confidence travels as the weakest input times the rule's strength, and fades with time. The system never sounds more certain than its ground.

The four layers

From the ground to the singers.

Four layers form one path. Trust and data flow up from the substrate; conclusions flow back down, each carrying its trail. Read it from the red dust upward.

Layer 04 · the singers

CrystalMind

Four named stances — TruthSeeker, Guardian, Visionary, Creator — that act only when they're called. None of them runs on its own or wakes another. The Guardian inspects a conclusion's real coherence and provenance and removes what doesn't stand, no matter how confident the voice that proposed it.

Layer 03 · the tending

CrystalEvolve

Population-based training of hybrid genomes — numbers and symbolic rules together — grown from a fixed seed, so a run reproduces exactly. Symbolic validity is a hard pass/fail gate, and high family-priority survivors are protected when memory runs tight.

Layer 02 · the path

CrystalFlow

Symbolic reasoning you can read one step at a time, with a small autograd engine underneath. Chains stop at the first denial, and confidence and provenance are carried the whole way — so the route and its certainty are always legible.

Layer 01 · the ground

CrystalMemory

A sparse, consent-aware store. Every node holds provenance, coherence, consent flags and family priority alongside its data. Persistence is atomic and checksum-protected — safe on a microSD card — and the store prunes itself to stay within a hard memory limit.

Why it's built this way

A mirror, not an oracle.

CrystalCore doesn't hand down answers from somewhere you can't see. It reflects your own reasoning back to you — step by step, source by source — and when a conclusion stands on thin ground, the Guardian says so plainly.

The point was never to build something to be obeyed. It was to build something you can see straight through: an intelligence that lives close to you, remembers where its knowledge came from, and stays honest about how far it can be trusted.

Where it stands

Built today, and on the way.

An honest map — what works now, and what's still ahead. Like everything here, a foundation laid in the open.

Built today

Working and tested

  • All four layers, integrated into one reason → remember → improve loop
  • 118 passing tests, including seven cross-cutting invariants
  • Zero required dependencies — runs on bare Python 3
  • Two end-to-end demos: a family decision, and a policy council
On the way

Next to be laid

A note on honesty

The mystical language of Songlines to Starlines belongs to the art — the concept work, the music, the stories. CrystalCore itself is plain engineering: the claims on this page describe what the code actually does today. The vision is the direction we're singing toward; the code is the ground we're standing on. Both are real. Neither is pretending to be the other.
