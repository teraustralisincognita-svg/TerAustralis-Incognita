Get Involved

Help dream a new Australia into reality.

Sovereign space capability isn't built by one person or one company — it's built by a coalition. If any part of this is yours to carry, we'd like to hear from you.

Who we're looking for

There's a role here for many kinds of people.

Whether you set policy, move minerals, build hardware, hold knowledge of Country, or back ambitious things early — there is a place for you in this.

Government & policy

Departments, agencies, advisers and elected representatives who understand that sovereign capability is a national interest worth backing.

Industry & critical minerals

Miners, refiners, processors and materials specialists — especially those working on rare earths, reprocessing and onshore value-adding.

Space & engineering

Engineers, scientists, founders and operators across launch, satellites, robotics, manufacturing and the wider space supply chain.

Traditional Owners & community

First Nations communities and organisations whose knowledge, consent and partnership are foundational to doing this properly — not an afterthought.

Investors & partners

Patient capital and strategic partners who want to back sovereign capability for the long term — and who value getting it right over getting it fast.

Media & storytellers

Journalists, writers and communicators who can help Australians see what's possible — and hold the project to the honest standard it sets for itself.
