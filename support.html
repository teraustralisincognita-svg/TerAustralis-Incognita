Transparency

Every dollar, in the open.

We use Open Collective so our finances are public by default. Anyone can see what comes in, what goes out, and why. For a project asking people to believe in it, that openness is the point.

  • A public ledger of income and spending.
  • Receipts attached to expenses.
  • No hidden cuts — just platform and payment fees.
  • Honest about being early-stage, every step of the way.
